Our Story 📖

From one tiny kitchen to your favourite food app.

Cooked with love, delivered with care ❤️

FreshBite started in 2020 as a single cloud kitchen in Bandra, Mumbai, with one simple belief: great food shouldn't have to wait. What began with three cooks and a scooter is now a family of 8 partner restaurants serving thousands of happy foodies.

Every kitchen on FreshBite is handpicked, hygiene-audited, and obsessed with freshness. We source local ingredients each morning, pack meals in eco-friendly boxes, and get them to your door while they're still steaming.

Whether it's a lazy Sunday biryani, a midnight brownie emergency, or a healthy weekday bowl — we've got a kitchen for every craving.
